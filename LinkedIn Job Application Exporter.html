Job Application Export Hub

Convert your job application history from LinkedIn, TheLadders, or Indeed into a clean CSV.

Instruction for TheLadders:

Go to your Applied Jobs page, highlight the application rows with your mouse, copy them, and paste them below. Our parser will automatically separate titles, companies, and dates.

Official LinkedIn Export

Use this if you have requested your Applied Jobs.csv from LinkedIn Settings.

How to request:

  1. LinkedIn > Settings > Data Privacy
  2. Get a copy of your data
  3. Select "Applied Jobs"
  4. Wait for email (approx 24h)
